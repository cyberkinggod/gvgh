Frequently Asked Questions

A PSN account with games is a PlayStation Network account that has purchased digital games. When you buy one of our accounts, you get access to all the games that have been purchased on that account. You can download and play these games on your own console.

Primary Account: You can set this as the primary account on your PS4/PS5, allowing all users on your console to access the games. You can also play the games from your own account.

Secondary Account: You'll need to log into this account to download and play the games. Only this account can access the games, but you can play them on your console.

After your payment is confirmed, you'll receive an email with the account details (email and password) and step-by-step instructions on how to access and download the games. For instant delivery items, this usually happens within minutes.

We strongly recommend against changing any account details. These accounts are shared with multiple customers, and changing details will lock others out, which violates our terms of service and may result in account suspension.

We guarantee all accounts for 30 days from purchase. If you lose access within this period, contact our support team with your order details, and we'll provide a replacement account with similar games.
